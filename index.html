F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with the Smart Dine system?

Getting started is simple — we provide a one-time portal setup for your restaurant with QR-based table menus, order tracking, and admin access. No physical installation or visit is required; everything is managed remotely through the portal.

Is the ₹799 subscription fee fixed?

The monthly subscription fee generally starts around ₹799 but can vary depending on your restaurant's size, number of tables, and specific features you need. Custom plans can be discussed during onboarding.

Can I see a demo before purchasing?

Yes! We offer a live walkthrough of the system and dashboard. Just reach out, and we’ll send you a demo link to explore the platform in action — no obligations.

Can I request custom features or branding?

Absolutely! We offer customized design, feature additions, or branding (like your restaurant's logo and theme). Additional charges may apply based on complexity.

What kind of support is included?

Our system is designed to be easy to manage, but in case you need help, we offer online support for any technical issues, updates, or changes — no in-person visit required.

Do I need any special hardware or mobile app?

No additional hardware or mobile app is needed. Customers scan the QR code from their table and access your menu directly in their browser. Orders appear in your admin panel in real time.
